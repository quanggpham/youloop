# Privacy Policy for YouLoop

**Last updated:** 2026-06-23

## Data Collection

YouLoop does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## What We Store

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's `storage.local` API:

| Data | Purpose | Sent anywhere? |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Loop start/end timestamps | Remember your A–B loop points per video | No |
| Extension enabled/disabled state | Remember if you've toggled the extension off | No |

## How Data Is Used

- Loop points are saved so your settings persist when you revisit a video
- The enabled/disabled toggle state is remembered across browser restarts
- All data stays on your device — it is never sent to any server or third party

## Third Parties

This extension does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The Ko-fi link in the popup opens `ko-fi.com/dzungquangpham` in a new tab — no data is sent until you choose to click it.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| `storage` | Save loop points and preferences locally |
| `*://*.youtube.com/*` | Inject the loop UI into YouTube's video player |

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out via [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/dzungquangpham).

## Changes

We will update this policy if the extension's data practices change. Check back on this page for updates.
